# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

This project is committed to a respectful, professional, and inclusive collaboration environment.

## Expected Behavior

- Communicate with clarity and respect.
- Focus feedback on technical outcomes and project goals.
- Assume positive intent and resolve disagreements constructively.
- Keep discussions evidence-based and action-oriented.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, abusive language, or personal attacks.
- Discrimination based on personal characteristics.
- Publicly sharing private information without consent.
- Repeated disruptive behavior that blocks collaboration.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards.
Violations may result in warning, content moderation, or removal from collaboration channels.

## Reporting

For conduct concerns, open a private security advisory or contact the maintainer through repository channels.
